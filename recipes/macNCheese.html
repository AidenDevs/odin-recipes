Mac N Cheese

Description

This is a mac-n-cheese recipe. Only the freshest ingredients should be used in order to make the freshest mac!

Once finished, the mac should be savory and creamy.

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces elbow macaroni
  • 4 ounces cheese
  • 2 fluid ounces milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add pasta and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
  3. Place a saucepan over medium-low heat.
  4. Combine the cheese food, milk, and pepper in the saucepan; cook until the cheese has melted, stirring frequently.
  5. Stir in the drained macaroni until evenly coated.
