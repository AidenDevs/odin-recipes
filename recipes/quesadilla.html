Quesadilla

Description

This is an apple pie recipe. Only the freshest of apples should be used in order to make the freshest pie!

Once finished, the crust should be a crispy golden brown.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mashed potatoes
  • 6 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup sliced green onions
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons softened butter
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Stir together mashed potatoes, bacon, and green onions. Spread mashed potato mixture onto 4 tortillas
  2. Sprinkle shredded Cheddar cheese on top of mashed potatoes and top with remaining tortillas.
  3. Lightly butter each side of quesadilla.
  4. Preheat a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Place a quesadilla into the preheated pan; cook until golden brown and crispy, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Repeat with remaining quesadillas.
  6. Serve with salsa and sour cream.
